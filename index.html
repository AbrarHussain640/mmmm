Karachi Biryani

  • Rice , Chicken , water , pan , Gas
  • Prepare Rice: Rinse the rice and soak it for 30 minutes. Cook with whole spices and salt until 70% done. Marinate Meat: Mix meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and lemon juice. Let it marinate for at least an hour. Cook Meat: In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and sauté onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add marinated meat and cook until meat is tender.

Chicken Karahi

  • Chicken (cut into pieces) , Tomatoes (chopped) , Ginger-garlic paste , Green chilies (slit) . Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Heat oil in a karahi (wok) and sauté ginger-garlic paste until fragrant. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, and cook until tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate. Season with salt and cook until the chicken is tender and fully cooked. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Gulab Jamun

  • Milk powder , All-purpose flour , Baking powder , Ghee (clarified butter) , Sugar
  • Prepare Dough: Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and ghee. Knead into a soft dough with milk. Shape Balls: Form small, smooth balls from the dough, ensuring there are no cracks. Prepare Syrup: Dissolve sugar in water and boil to make a light syrup. Add a few cardamom pods for flavor. Fry Balls: Heat ghee in a pan and fry the dough balls on low heat until golden brown. Soak in Syrup: Immerse the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least an hour before serving

Gulab Jamun

  • Boneless meat (chicken, beef, or mutton) , Mixed lentils (chana dal, masoor dal, moong dal) , Cracked wheat (dalia) , Ginger-garlic paste , Fried onions
  • Cook Meat: In a large pot, cook boneless meat with ginger-garlic paste, salt, and spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric) until tender. Shred the meat. Cook Grains: Simultaneously, cook mixed lentils and cracked wheat until soft and mushy. Blend Mixture: Combine the cooked meat, lentils, and wheat. Blend the mixture using an immersion blender until smooth. Simmer Together: In the pot, add the blended mixture and additional spices. Cook on low heat, stirring frequently, until the consistency thickens. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fried onions, fresh coriander, green chilies, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve hot with naan or parathas.
